The year is 2172.

You are an employee of famed restaraunt chain Subway at a new location on a mining facility
in the asteroid belt. The work is hard. You are not paid well. At night the miners make fun of your hat.
You and several other employees decide to unionize to reclaim your dignity from the beastly Subway Corporation.
From earth, Subway learns of this attempt to unionize. They retaliate and take your grandmother hostage.
Later that day, the first lasergram arrives from earth with Subway's demands. You click 'accept' and listen.
Here begins our story of galactic workers' rights...
