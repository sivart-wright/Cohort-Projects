The Real History of Subway: "On this day..."

On this day, in 2059, cosmic nemesis Subway began requiring 17 hour work days
from employees. After a short period of armed resistance, 93 former employees disappeared.
In a press conference, Subway stated that their dissapearance was not connected to the recent
skirmish. As a gesture of good favor, Subway offered to contribute 930 dollars to the Subway Fund
in their memory. Critics suggest Subway was indeed behind the dissapearance, but news outlets are
unwilling to to report this claim. In the last 20 years, dozens of reporters have gone missing
after making similar claims about Subway's brutal employee policies. Subway is slated to begin
construction on their one-millionth restaraunt this fall, on Saturn.
